Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 9, 2026

Welcome to 7 Diamond VIP ("the App"). We are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

When you use the App, we may collect:

  • Name and email address (when you register or sign in)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Create and manage your game account

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services for authentication. Each has its own Privacy Policy:

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties for marketing purposes. Your data is used only to operate the App.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. Passwords are encrypted and never stored in plain text. All data is transmitted over secure HTTPS connections.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account data as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
